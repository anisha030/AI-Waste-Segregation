How It Works

A four-stage pipeline from raw image to actionable disposal guidance.

01
📸
Image Capture
Waste item photographed via webcam or file upload. Images are validated and cleaned for truncation errors before processing.
02
⚙️
Preprocessing
Resized to 224×224, normalised to [−1,1] for MobileNetV2, augmented during training with flips, rotations and brightness shifts.
03
🧠
AI Classification
Groq Vision AI analyses the actual image and predicts one of 10 waste classes with a confidence score — no keyword guessing.
04
🗺️
Disposal Guidance
Predicted class maps to specific disposal instructions — correct bin, recycling tips, hazard warnings — reducing environmental harm.
